LightWAVE bug list

  • Signals not visible/not centered.
    There are at least four bugs that cause this symptom:
    • In records that have no signals (e.g., crisdb records) a null response from the server is not handled properly in the client.
    • In records with incorrectly specified adczero (e.g., qtdb/sel100), the signals may be rendered off-screen.
    • In records with sampling frequencies less than 0.1 Hz, there is at most one sample in the window, and nothing is drawn as a result.
    • If the signal name contains a "+" (or other characters that have special meanings in query strings, such as "#", "%", "?", "/", "&", "!") the signal name will be mangled in transit to the server, and the server won't retrieve samples of it.
  • LightWAVE doesn't work in Internet Explorer.
    Yes, it does. But unless you have IE 9 or 10, you must install the free Google Chrome Frame plugin.
  • Can't go to times that are not multiples of 10 seconds.
  • MIMIC II Waveform Database "numerics" records are missing.
    LightWAVE needs a record submenu, like the PhysioBank ATM has.
